WOMXN is a varied and welcoming environment where womxn and non binary peeps can work, belong and thrive. At WOMXN, we are working hard to create a welcoming environment where our members may be seen, heard, and empowered to succeed in their social, economic, and personal lives.
Within our premises, we hope you will pursue meaningful work, whatever that means to you. Our purpose is to create a diverse and inclusive world in which women have access to the resources they need to grow their businesses and make a long-term impact in their communities.
We do so by focusing on professional development and networking, as well as promoting mental health and wellness, creativity, and social gatherings.
